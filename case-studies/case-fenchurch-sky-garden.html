N° 11 — EC3 Fenchurch Restaurant · Sky Garden
(01) — The brief

A luxury restaurant floor, restored overnight. Ready every morning.

Working within one of London's most iconic dining destinations, we delivered a complete timber floor refurbishment that elevated the restaurant's luxury interior. Our specialists meticulously sanded the existing flooring, applied a bespoke black wood stain, and finished the surface with a durable protective coating.

The deep black timber flooring provides a timeless, contemporary aesthetic while offering the resilience required for a busy, high-end hospitality environment. The finished result is a seamless blend of craftsmanship, durability and style, enhancing the overall guest experience at Fenchurch Restaurant.

A specialist floor finish, built for service

The existing timber floor was professionally sanded and restored before receiving a bespoke black wood stain and a commercial-grade protective coating. The specification was selected to create a refined contemporary finish while delivering the durability required in a high-footfall restaurant.
