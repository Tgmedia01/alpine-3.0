(01) — The brief

A calm fitness environment, formed inside an office building. Ready for daily practice.

Alpine MI delivered the main-contractor fit-out for Yoga Haven in Croydon, transforming 1,800 ft² of office accommodation into a dedicated leisure and fitness space.

The ten-week programme brought together the principal studio, changing facilities, lockers and supporting areas, creating a practical venue with a clear and welcoming identity.
